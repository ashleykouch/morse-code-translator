A Little About Morse Code

A method of telecommunication using different signal durations with dots and dashes (or dits and dahs), the codes are transmitted as electrical pulses or visual signals such as flashing lighted.

Morse code was a critical form of communication during World War II and was used as an internation standard for sea communication until 1999.
